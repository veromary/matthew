Matthew

This project is based on Fr Lauer's plan for reading the Bible in a year.

The Gospels are read through twice - Matthew's Gospel begins in December for Advent and then again in June. Each reading should have two dates.

St Paul's Centre for Biblical Studies has two courses on Matthew's Gospel:

  1. Reading the Old Testament in the New: The Gospel of Matthew a written course in 6 lessons.
  2. Proclaiming the Kingdom: The Gospel of Matthew an audio course of 10 lectures.

The second breaks up the chapters so I'll use that as a guide to my sections here.
